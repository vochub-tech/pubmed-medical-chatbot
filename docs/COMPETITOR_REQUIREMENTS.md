## Research Questions

### Product Features
- [ ] What features do they offer?
- [ ] How do they handle citations/sources?
- [ ] What's their accuracy/trust model?
- [ ] Do they use MeSH/UMLS?
- [ ] How do they handle disclaimers?

### User Experience
- [ ] Target audience (patients vs clinicians)?
- [ ] Input method (chat, symptoms, structured)?
- [ ] Output format (text, links, recommendations)?
- [ ] Mobile/web/app distribution?

### Business Model
- [ ] Pricing (free/freemium/paid/B2B)?
- [ ] Revenue model (subscription, per-query, enterprise)?
- [ ] Customer segments?

### Competitive Position
- [ ] What do they do well?
- [ ] What's missing (our opportunity)?
- [ ] Barriers to entry?
- [ ] Network effects?

### Regulatory/Legal
- [ ] FDA clearance status (if any)?
- [ ] Patent/IP landscape?
- [ ] Liability disclaimers?

## Our Differentiation

**Current unique value proposition:**
1. **Direct PubMed integration** - Real citations, not hallucinated
2. **MeSH term mapping** - Accurate medical terminology
3. **Open source** - Transparent methodology
4. **MCP integration** - Works with AI assistants

**Questions for Scout:**
- Is this differentiation meaningful?
- What should we add?
- What's our biggest competitive threat?
